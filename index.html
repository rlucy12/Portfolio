Crypto Price Prediction

Extensive research was conducted to construct a suite of very accurate prediction models, which included LSTM, RNN, ARIMA, CNN-LSTM Hybrid, and ARIMA-CNN-LSTM Fusion. To enhance performance, LSTM, RNN, and ARIMA-CNN-LSTM models were iterated continuously, with rigorous testing procedures and hyperparameter fine-tuning. Collaborated closely with the research team to provide new ideas and improve testing procedures. After discovering flaws in the ARIMA, CNN, and LSTM ensembles, we investigated Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN), resulting in the development of a fusion model combining TCN and LSTM. This novel approach dramatically improved bitcoin prediction accuracy, advancing our study goal of producing superior forecasting results.
